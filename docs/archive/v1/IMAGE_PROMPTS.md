## Style Consistency Notes

**For all backgrounds:**
- Match the warm, golden hour lighting of existing Forest/Rocky/Coastal
- Photorealistic with slight painterly quality
- 16:9 or 21:9 aspect ratio for full-bleed sections
- Rich but not oversaturated colors
- Should feel like same photographer/artist created them

**For wildlife:**
- Pure white backgrounds for easy transparency masking
- Consistent illustration style across all animals
- Detailed but not hyper-realistic (should feel like naturalist illustrations)
